StockPulse: Forecast & Alerts

The InventoryForecastDashboard is a custom Odoo 18 OWL (Owl Web Library) component that provides a rich, multi-tab business intelligence interface for inventory, purchasing, manufacturing, and product analytics.

1.Demand Chart: Visualizes sales demand per product using Pie, Bar, or Doughnut charts. You can filter by date range and limit to Top 5 or 8 products. Data exports to XLSX.

2.Purchase Suggestions: Shows products that have fallen below their reorder point and need restocking. Groups suggestions by vendor and lets you auto-create draft purchase orders with one click..

3. Dead Stock Identifies products sitting in your warehouse with zero sales in the past 12 months. Classifies them by severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low) based on their on-hand value..

4.Seasonal Forecast Calculates monthly seasonal demand patterns from the past 24 months of sales history. Forecasts the next 12 months and highlights which months are historically above or below average

5.Purchase Suggestions: Shows products that have fallen below their reorder point and need restocking. Groups suggestions by vendor and lets you auto-create draft purchase orders with one click..

6. Product Analytics Breaks down each product's Revenue, Tax, COGS, and Gross Margin %. Products with margins below 20% are flagged. Full data exports to XLSX.

7.Manufacturing

7.1. Production Orders: status of all MOs with overdue detection

7.2. BoM Stock Check: shows how many units you can produce right now with current stock

7.3. Component Shortages: flags raw materials that are short for active manufacturing orders
