Account Reconcilation

Make account reconciliation in Odoo Community faster and more reliable, ensuring your invoices and payments are properly matched with minimal effort.

1.Navigate to the Accounting menu for quick and easy access.

2.Navigate to the Accounting menu, then select the Reconcile submenu.

3.Here you will see the journals that need to be reconciled, grouped by partners.

4.Open any one of the journals.

5.Select multiple journals that require reconciliation. Once selected, the Reconcile button will appear at the top.Click on that.

6.A wizard will appear, allowing you to select the appropriate account. Click the Reconcile button to reconcile the selected journals efficiently.
